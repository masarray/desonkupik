# Code of Conduct

DeSonKuPik aims to be a welcoming project for creators, engineers, and audio enthusiasts.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent when possible.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Give clear bug reports and actionable feedback.
- Welcome beginners and non-native English speakers.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Spam, scams, or unrelated promotion.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who repeatedly harm the project community.

## Reporting

Report serious conduct concerns through the project maintainer channels.
