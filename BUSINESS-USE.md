# Business Use

This page explains common business scenarios in simple language.

## Can a company use DeSonKuPik?

Yes. A company may use the official app internally, subject to the repository license and release notes.

## Can freelancers and agencies use it for client work?

Yes. You may use the official app as a creative tool in your workflow.

## Can I redistribute DeSonKuPik?

Redistribution must follow the repository license. If you distribute modified builds, be clear that they are modified and avoid confusing users with official SonKuPik releases.

## Can I use the official DeSonKuPik name and logo?

Normal reference is allowed. For product branding, resale, OEM, white-label, or modified app distribution under official-looking branding, see `TRADEMARK.md` and contact SonKuPik.

## When do I need a separate business arrangement?

Contact SonKuPik for:

- OEM bundling.
- White-label versions.
- Enterprise support.
- Custom presets or DSP tuning.
- Commercial use of official branding.
- Modified builds distributed as a product.
